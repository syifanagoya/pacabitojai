    Is Central Cee gay rumors true? Get insights on the rapper’s split reasons with his girlfriend, Madeline Argy.

    Central Cee, the rising British rapper behind the viral hit “Doja,” has been making headlines for his music and his personal life.

    The 23-year-old artist, whose real name is Courtney Freckleton, has been dating TikTok star Madeline Argy since last year, but their relationship has been shrouded in mystery and speculation.

    Here’s everything we know about their romance, the gay rumors, and the reason for their split.

    Is Central Cee Gay? The Truth Behind The Gay Rumors And The Breakup

    One of the most controversial lyrics in Central Cee’s song “Doja” is “How can I be homophobic? My bitch is gay”.

    The line sparked confusion among fans, who wondered if he was implying that he was gay or bisexual himself or if he was dating a woman who was.

    In an interview with Genius, Central Cee clarified the meaning behind the lyric, saying that his girlfriend at the time was bisexual and that he was not homophobic.

    “It’s literally what I say like I’m not homophobic; my girlfriend is bisexual. She was like the first proper gay person I communicated with.

    I feel like homophobia is almost embedded into the culture, like rap music, even just the hood. It’s quite known; it’s embedded in us to like shun upon, which I don’t think is right,” he said.

    The girlfriend he was referring to was Madeline Argy, a popular TikToker with over 2 million followers. Madeline has previously mentioned that she had an ex-girlfriend in some of her videos, confirming her sexuality.

    Central Cee and Madeline Argy’s Relationship Timeline

    Central Cee and Madeline Argy started dating in mid-2022, but they kept their relationship low-key and did not publicly confirm it until later.

    However, fans noticed some clues that suggested they were an item, such as wearing each other’s clothes and being spotted together in public.

    In June 2022, Central Cee posted a series of photos on Instagram from his trip to Morocco, where he wore a blue hoodie.

    A few weeks later, Madeline uploaded a TikTok video where she wore what appeared to be the same hoodie. Fans also found footage of Central Cee exiting a building with a girl who looked like Madeline following him.

    In August 2022, Madeline made a cryptic TikTok video where she wore a t-shirt that said, “I love lying,” and said: “I have literally never heard of Central Cee in my entire life; I’m just as confused as you are.” In the background of the video, Central Cee’s voice could be heard saying, “Babe.”
