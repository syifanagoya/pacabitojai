    Brooke Logan is a name that’s synonymous with The Bold and the Beautiful. She’s been a part of the show since it began, and Katherine Kelly Lang is one of only two of the original actors still on. The other, of course, is John McCook, who’s been heading the Forrester family since day one. Now, though, Eric’s facing his own mortality and fighting tooth and nail against ageism, insisting that he’s just as creative, powerful and important as ever at Forrester Creations.

    More: They wouldn’t really kill Eric, would they?

    Brooke, meanwhile, is busy showing us that the more things change, the more they stay the same as she prepares to re-marry Ridge after their latest reunion. It’s nearly impossible to think of Bold & Beautiful without McCook or Lang, but it’s a day that must inevitably come. Or is it? That may not be something we’ll have to worry about with Lang! Brooke’s longtime portrayer was Down Under, making an appearance on Australia’s Studio 10, when she was asked if she was planning on sticking around for the 40th anniversary in four years.

    In response, Lang waved her hand. “Oh, yes,” she exclaimed. “Maybe the 50th! The 60th! I love the show. I’m really grateful to be there. I just can’t believe after 36 years I still love it more — even more! Actually, I love it even more than I ever have.”

    So there you have it. Brooke’s going to be around another four years, 14 years and even 24 years if Lang has her way! And we’re guessing that means the “Bridge” merry-go-round will keep on going so long as Ridge is around to back her up!

    The show, as Lang put it, is “just a blessing to me. I feel like I’ve grown up there and I will stay as long as I possibly can.”

    And for that, we couldn’t be happier!
