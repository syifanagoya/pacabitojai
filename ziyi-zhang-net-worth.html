    FAQs about Ziyi Zhang

    Who is Ziyi Zhang?

    She is a renowned Chinese actress and model, famous for her roles in both Chinese and international films. She gained international fame for her role in Crouching Tiger, Hidden Dragon (2000) and has starred in several Hollywood films including Memoirs of a Geisha (2005) and Rush Hour 2 (2001).

    What is Zhang’s educational background?

    She studied dance at the Beijing Dance Academy starting at age 11 and later attended the prestigious Central Academy of Drama in Beijing to study acting.

    What was Zhang’s breakthrough role?

    Her breakthrough role was in the film Crouching Tiger, Hidden Dragon (2000), directed by Ang Lee, which brought her international acclaim.

    What are some of Zhang’s most famous movies?

    Some of her most famous movies include Hero (2002), House of Flying Daggers (2004), Memoirs of a Geisha (2005), and The Grandmaster (2013).

    How many awards has Zhang won?

    She has won numerous awards, including 12 Best Actress awards for The Grandmaster (2013), making her the most awarded Chinese actress for a single film.

    Has she worked in Hollywood?

    Yes, she has worked in Hollywood films such as Rush Hour 2 (2001), Memoirs of a Geisha (2005), and Godzilla: King of the Monsters (2019).

    What languages does she speak?

    Ziyi primarily speaks Mandarin Chinese and has also worked in English-language films.

    Is Ziyi Zhang married?

    Yes, she is married to Chinese rock musician Wang Feng. They have a daughter and a son together.

    What was Zhang’s role in Memoirs of a Geisha?

    In Memoirs of a Geisha (2005), she played the lead role of Sayuri, a young girl who becomes one of Japan’s most celebrated geishas.

    What recent projects has she been involved in?
