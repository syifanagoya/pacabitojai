    As the founder of the cashmere brand Guest In Residence, Gigi Hadid knows a thing or two about knitwear staples — including how to spice them up. Case in point? Over the weekend, the model decided to take one of her brand’s cardigans from simple to sexy for both a girls’ night out and a date night by pairing it with leather boots and … not much else.

    On Saturday, Hadid was first spotted wearing the hot fall look in question when departing from a star-studded dinner in New York City alongside Selena Gomez, Taylor Swift, Sophie Turner, Cara Delevingne, and Brittany Mahomes. Proving that she’s firmly planted in her Reputation era, Gigi sported Guest in Residence’s black Industry Jacket (which she wore buttoned-up to mimic the feel of a micro-minidress) during the outing paired with classic black leather knee-high boots. A small black handbag and dainty silver drop earrings accessorized the A-lister’s ‘fit, and she rounded out her moody look by pulling her blonde hair back into a sleek updo.

    Shortly after grabbing dinner with her girls, Hadid opted to keep the good times going by meeting up with Bradley Cooper in NYC while wearing the very same outfit. While the pair have yet to confirm their romance publicly, their outing comes shortly after they embarked on several dates together both in and outside of the city throughout the past month.

    "They are having fun,” a source told People about Cooper and Hadid back in Oct. “[They have] things in common so it’s possible to see it progress. It appears super casual now, but they both have kids, big careers, busy lives and understand what life is like in these circles. It’s cute…and there is an attraction."
