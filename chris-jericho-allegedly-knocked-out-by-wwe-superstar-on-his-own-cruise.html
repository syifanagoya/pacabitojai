    Chris Jericho Allegedly Knocked Out By WWE Superstar on His Own Cruise

    Chris Jericho lives his life like a rock star, and that is not a gimmick. Now, it appears we have new information about one of those rock star moments, but it didnt end well for Jericho on his own cruise.

    16 Apr 2024, 00:00

    Chris Jericho lives his life like a rock star, and that is not a gimmick. Now, it appears we have new information about one of those rock star moments, but it didn’t end well for Jericho on his own cruise.

    As we previously reported, Brian Last said on an episode of the “Cornette’s Drive Thru” podcast that he had a story about Jericho getting KO’ed on his own cruise.

    “Man, there is a story I would love to tell, but i didn’t get permission, but him getting knocked out on his own cruise ship by someone he mouthed off to. It’s the most incredible f*cking story, but I can’t talk about that today.“

    Bodyslam.net noted a bit more information about this situation, and what went down. Sources reportedly were able to confirm the story, along with the identity of the person involved.

    The altercation occurred during Chris Jericho’s Rock N’ Wrestling Rager At Sea – Part Deux: Second Wave in January 2020. Chris Jericho and former WWE US and Tag Champion MVP had a heated exchange that resulted in MVP knocking out Chris Jericho.

     The incident took place during Chris Jericho’s Rock N’ Wrestling Rager At Sea – Part Deux: Second Wave, back in January 2020. During the cruise there was an altercation between Jericho and former WWE US and Tag Champion MVP, where heated words were exchanged that resulted in MVP knocking out Chris Jericho.

    This encounter wasn’t the only time the two crossed paths. Another incident took place a year or two after the cruise, this time in a hotel lobby. Sources also reportedly confirmed this incident, describing “a heated exchange” that led to Jericho quickly heading to the elevator. As the door closed, he reportedly said, “I don’t fight jobbers.”

    Chris Jericho has been in the news a lot recently, but it seems that AEW will keep going, booking Y2J in a prime spot on the roster, despite that allegations about Kylie Rae floating around. Now, we have another story, and a name to go with it. This time, it was MVP that apparently KO’ed The Wizard.

    We will keep our eye on this story, and so much more here at Ringside News. As of this writing, Chris Jericho has not replied to this new story.
