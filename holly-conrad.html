    Holly Conrad (born May 22, 1986) is an American YouTube Star, Cosplayer, Businesswoman, and Special FX Artist from Los Angeles, California. She is famous for her YouTube channel “Commander Holly” having 374+ k subscribers.

    Furthermore, the 33-year-old player uploads content related to Games, Vlogs, does Crafting, Cosplay Tutorials, etc. As a cosplayer, she gained fame for creating “Commander Shepard” from the Mass Effect series created by Bioware.

    Moreover, Holly was Bioware’s official Commander Shepard in 2011 and 2012. She also designed various outfits based on the series including Grunt and Wrex. As a special FX artist, Conrad’s notable work includes inventing a Kaiju for the promotional video Pacific Rim – Training Day.

    In fact, she also created multiple creatures for a series of videos titled “The Four Players”. In addition, the YouTube star is also the members of a DnD group; Dice, Camera, Action, along with Nathan Sharp, Jared Knabenbauer, Anna Prosser Robinson, etc that shows every Tuesday on Twitch.

    Personal Affairs, Boyfriend & Spouse

    The 33-year-old Holly was married to her husband “Ross O’Donovan”. He is an Australian animator, voice actor, and Internet personality. The ex-couple tied the knot on September 29, 2012. They also dated each other for a while before their marriage.

    Furthermore, on September 19, 2018, Ross and Holly announced about their divorce on their respective Twitter account. The main reason for their separation was that Conrad moved to Seattle, Washington.

    Later, the YouTube star began her affair with her boyfriend Jared “ProJared” Knabenbauer (a famous YouTube gamer). It is not clear when their relationship started. But Jared was wedded to his wife “Heidi O’Ferrall” at that time.

    Recently, Heidi revealed that her spouse had cheated on her and had an extra-marital affair with another gamer i.e. Holly. In fact, more than 100,000 fans already unsubscribed from Knabenbauer’s popular YouTube channel after the revelation.

    Moreover, Jared also accused of soliciting nudes from minor fans. Conrad also switched all of her social media accounts to private after the news broke. Holly’s subscribers also started to unfollow her from YouTube, Instagram, and Twitter.
