    Yvonne Suhor (born November 29, 1965) is an actress from the United States. Suhor was born in the Louisiana city of New Orleans. She earned her bachelor’s degree from Illinois State University in 1985, and her master’s degree in fine arts from the University of Southern California. With a diverse range of roles in films and television shows, Suhor is perhaps best known for her role as Cicely in an Emmy Award-winning episode of Northern Exposure, as well as her role as Louise McCloud on The Young Riders, which she played for three seasons. Additionally, she has appeared in Brooklyn Bridge (in a recurring role), Murder, She Wrote, Star Trek: Voyager, Renegade, and Sheena, among other television programmes. Suhor also has a number of theatrical credits to her resume, including Steppenwolf’s Grapes of Wrath and Lydie Breeze, and she has been nominated for a Jeff Award on two separate occasions. Suhor now owns and operates her own Art’s Sake Acting Studios in Orlando, Florida, where she teaches Core Film Acting in accordance with the Meisner method.
