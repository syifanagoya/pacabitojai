      Nick Jonas is often at the centre of a romance rumour and this time he has been linked to actress Lily Collins. The new couple have reportedly been spending time together in recent weeks but it seems it's not as serious as people may think.

      Nick is rumoured to be dating Lily

      Nick and his new beau have enjoyed several dates and a source told that the American singer has been spending a lot of time with the British beauty. "They like each other, but it's new and not serious yet," the insider said. "They are having fun together."

      In January they were seen laughing while bowling and a week later they were pictured grabbing some lunch with mutual friend, Demi Lovato, in West Hollywood. According to , they even hit the slopes together in Mammoth Mountain Ski Area, California.

      The pair have been spotted together

      However, a second insider told the publication: "Nick is single and having fun," and that the pair are not exclusive.

      Nick was also linked to Kate Hudson when the two reportedly enjoyed a very brief romance at the end of September. "It was just a fling," a source close to Kate told . "It's definitely not anything serious."

      The pair enjoyed lunch with mutual friend Demi Lovato

      The 23-year-old songwriter, who split with former Miss USA Olivia Culpo last year, seems to be enjoying a single lifestyle.

      Speaking of his break up with Olivia in 's latest issue, he said: "It was first excitement to have freedom to be whoever I wanted to be, be my own person".

      Lily rekindled her old flame with Jamie in May 2015

      "Then the feeling of being disheartened by what's out there. Then getting into crazy artist mode and throwing it all out, the good, bad, and ugly. Then meeting people, making a real effort to open up and be free as a person."

      Denying any serious relationship rumours, the star is focusing on meeting new people. "I've met some people that are amazing," he revealed. "And my current situation is that I'm very much single, but I'm trying to be as open to people who can inspire me in some way."

      Meanwhile, Lily rekindled her old flame with Jamie Campbell Bower in May 2015. The pair dated for a year, but the relationship ended in 2013. They announced their reunion via social media, with fans showing their love for the couple.
